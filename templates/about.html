About Better Sun

Our project is a web application that allows users to check the solar exposure of designated areas. It assists in selecting the most optimal locations for solar panels by displaying a heatmap that shows where the panels will operate most efficiently and generate the maximum amount of energy in kilowatt-hours (kWh). Additionally, the application helps users analyze how temperature or sunlight exposure on a given area might change due to environmental factors, ultimately aiding in better energy management in both residential and commercial environments. We aim for everyone—whether they are homeowners or solar company operators—to optimize their green energy production as effectively as possible, which will have a significant impact on mitigating environmental changes caused by inefficient energy management.
